The placeholder attribute

Placeholder used as a label or instructions

Can you spot the issues?

Placeholder text must not be used as a replacement for labels or instructions:

  • Not reliably available to all screen readers
  • Often low contrast
  • Disappears when typing

Name, role and current state

  • As a screen reader user
  • I want: to know the name, role and current state of a form control
  • So that: I know what I am interacting with and the current state (checked, collapsed etc) so that I do not generate an error
